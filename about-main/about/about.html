About us

Quality . Service . Value

Megha Marketing EA Ltd was incorporated in March 2011 and has grown to be known for its service proposition for quality goods at a competitive price. We are driven by meticulous research, understanding of various products in a customer's centric focus to provide the highest quality value proposition to the customer. We specialize in importing,sourcing and distributing quality steel,cement and construction chemicals,paints,swimming pool, water pumps/treatment & solars Water tanks, PVC, CPVC, PPR pipes & fittings and building materials. We stock over 10,0000 different products.

Mission

To provide quality goods with great value and excellent service.

Vision

To provide provide the market with a one stop shop solution for all building materials and to uplift standards for construction

Our Core Values

Uprightness

We speak openly and honestly and always act with integrity and the trust of our customers and patners so they can rely on us to keep our promises and do the right thing, even when it's hard.

Humbleness

We listen, learn and share to create value for our customers and patners.

Constant Care

Take care of today, actively prepare for tomorrow. We anticipate, innovate and strive to improve everything we do.

Our Employees

Creating a motivating, diverse and inclusive environment where our people feel safe, valued and empowered.

Our Name

Our name is a promise and a commitment to trust and excellence. We strive for a more sustainable and integrated world.
